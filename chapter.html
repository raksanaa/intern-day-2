Chapter 1 — The First Stone

They said the stones chose their keepers. On the last day of summer, Mira crossed the dry streambed and found a faintly glowing stone half buried in dust. When she picked it up, the world felt slightly different—like the air itself had shifted around her footsteps.

Chapter 2 — The Map and the Market

Word of Mira’s stone spread through the market before she could take ten steps. A mapmaker handed her an old paper with a single X drawn on it—a place where another stone was said to sleep.

Chapter 3 — Between Two Fires

The second stone glowed warm beneath the rubble of a ruined mill. Mira began to understand that each stone revealed a story waiting to be finished, and that carrying one was both a gift and a responsibility.
